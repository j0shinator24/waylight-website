What we handle

The financial admin, handled for you

We take on invoices, budgets, claims, and provider payments so you can focus on your goals, your supports, and your life.

Invoice Processing

We receive provider invoices, verify them against current NDIS rates, and process payment. Reimbursements handled the same way.

Budget Tracking

Monthly statements with spending breakdowns by category. You always know where your plan balance stands.

Budget Alerts
